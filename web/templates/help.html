Defi Kingdoms Tax Helper

Guide to Lila's Transaction History Service

General Usage

  • Just put in your wallet address at the top on the homepage, click Generate, and wait to get your report.
  • After your report is generated, it will appear on two tabs. Default view is Tax Report, but you can click on the Transactions tab to see the detailed transaction data.
  • An Export CSV button will also appear after generation, clicking it will send you a comma separated data file of the data for the tab you are viewing.
  • Change the report options on the left to customize how the report is generated.

Report Options

  • Date Range: Only events in this range will be included in the report. Older transactions will still be searched to look for cost basis.
  • Cost Basis: Three options are provided to adjust how cost basis is discovered. If you are not familiar with these tax accounting related terms, the default choice of First in - First out is most commonly used.

Tax Report

  • Tax reporting requirements vary in different areas, but this report is meant to help you with accounting for things that are commonly required.
  • The Capital Gains section will list detected NFT and Token Sales, and attempt to calculate the cost basis/gains for them where possible.
  • The Income section will list detected events that are commonly categorized as income like bank interest and rewards.
  • The Expenses section will list various costs you have incurred that may be helpful in filling gaps in your cost basis in some situations.
  • If you see the warning icon in the costs column on the tax report, it means that Lila could not find a source for all of the cost basis of that item. You can find more details in the CSV export to see how much it was partially accounted if any.

Summary

  • The summary tab provides some summarized facts about your transactions.
  • These items are just informational curiousities and not necessarily useful for tax accounting.

Transaction Report

  • The transactions tab summarizes all activity for your wallet including things that are not included in the tax report.
  • Heroes: All records related to heroes like buy/sell/hire, summon, and level up.
  • Trader: Records of swaps make with trader or vendor in marketplace.
  • Liquidity: Records of swapping for LP seeds from the druid, and Entering and Withdrawing from liquidity pools.
  • Gardens: Events of claiming rewards from your LP seeds in the gardens.
  • Bank: Records of your bank deposits and withdrawals.
  • Airdrop: Any Jewel airdrops you have received.
  • Quests: All of your rewards from sending heroes on quests.
  • Wallet: Any miscellanous transfers to/from your wallet to non-game related addresses.

Price Data

  • Fiat Values are calculated based on token price at the end of day when the event happened.
  • Prices are sourced from Coin Gecko first if available, otherwise they are looked up from Defi Kingdoms graphql tokenDayData
  • For some non-denomination items like some quest rewards, prices may be based on current price if historical price data is empty on the DFK graph.
